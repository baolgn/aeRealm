Meet the Members of aespa

Karina

Position: Lead Dancer and Rapper
Full Name: Yoo Jimin
Birthdate: April 11, 2000
Fun Fact: Karina was the first revealed member of aespa and gained attention for her stunning visuals and dancing skills.

Winter

Position: Lead Vocalist
Full Name: Kim Minjeong
Birthdate: January 1, 2001
Fun Fact: Winter is known for her powerful vocals and made her debut as a member of aespa with the single "Black Mamba."

Giselle

Position: Main Rapper and Lead Vocalist
Full Name: Uchinaga Aeri
Birthdate: October 30, 2000
Fun Fact: Giselle is trilingual, being fluent in Korean, English, and Japanese. She lived in Japan and Canada during her childhood.

Ningning

Position: Lead Vocalist and Maknae (Youngest)
Full Name: Ning Yizhuo
Birthdate: October 23, 2002
Fun Fact: Ningning has a versatile vocal range and can sing in multiple languages, including Chinese and Korean.
