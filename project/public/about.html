Welcome to the World of aespa!

Welcome to the official fan haven for aespa, the sensational South Korean girl group brought to you by the creative geniuses at SM Entertainment! Since their spectacular debut on November 17, 2020, with the electrifying digital single "Black Mamba," aespa has been redefining the K-pop landscape with their unparalleled talent and mesmerizing performances.

Dive into the captivating world of aespa, where the very name holds a multitude of meanings, each adding a layer to the magic they bring to the stage. The iconic use of æ embodies the essence of "Avatar X Experience," inviting you to embark on a journey where you become an integral part of the immersive aespa universe. It's not just about being a spectator; it's about becoming an "aspect" and embracing both sides of the extraordinary.

In the enchanting realm of aespa, discover the profound significance of meeting another avatar of yourself, where every encounter unfolds into an exhilarating exploration of a new world. It's about transcending boundaries, facing fear and doubts head-on, and always delivering your best performance. It's a celebration of beauty and aesthetic, where aespa effortlessly embodies the essence of grace and artistic excellence.

Join us as we celebrate the boundless energy, talent, and passion of aespa – a group that goes beyond the ordinary, consistently pushing the boundaries of creativity and leaving an indelible mark on the hearts of fans worldwide. Welcome to the extraordinary journey that is aespa!


2020: The Dawn of aespa – Unveiling the Debut and the Cherished Fandom Name!

On October 26, 2020, the excitement soared as a captivating video graced aespa's Twitter, unveiling the very essence of the group – their name and the mesmerizing logo that would soon become iconic. With hearts racing, fans eagerly awaited the grand debut set for November, a promise of musical brilliance from the visionary minds at SM Entertainment.

October 27 marked the revelation of the first enchanting member, Winter, followed by the sensational Karina on October 28. The anticipation reached new heights as the pieces of the aespa puzzle fell into place, revealing a lineup that was destined for greatness.

During an extraordinary press conference on October 28, the legendary SM founder, Lee-Soo Man, shared the groundbreaking news – aespa would not only have four idol members but also four virtual members, crafting a future world that transcends boundaries between the real and virtual realms. A tantalizing teaser video showcased Winter, Karina, and the mystery members, Ningning and Giselle, alongside the ethereal æ-KARINA and the AI marvel, Naevis. October 29 witnessed the grand revelation of the third radiant member, Ningning, followed by the dazzling introduction of Giselle on October 30. The aespa family was complete, and the anticipation soared to unprecedented heights.

November 2 brought the thrilling announcement – aespa's debut with the digital single "Black Mamba" on November 17. A visual feast awaited as concept photos illuminated the path from November 4 to November 15. The crescendo of excitement reached its peak with the music video teaser gracing SMTOWN's YouTube channel on November 16.

On the monumental day of November 17, aespa's fandom was unveiled as "MY." Embodying the spirit of KWANGYA, where aespa's avatars reside, "MY" signifies 'The most precious friend,' weaving a beautiful connection between the group and their cherished supporters.


2021: aespa's Symphony of Triumphs – "Forever," "Next Level," Savage, and "Dreams Come True"!

Kicking off the year with a burst of excitement, Cheon-hoon of Sonic Korea Mastering Studio teased us with the ethereal comeback single "Forever" on January 21. The anticipation soared as aespa's official Twitter account echoed the promise with #I_will_always_stand_by_your_side. Soon after, on February 5, "Forever" was unveiled, a dazzling remake of Yoo Young Jin's timeless piece from S.M. Entertainment’s holiday album in 2000. Concept photos from February 1 to February 4 set the stage for an enchanting spectacle.

The accolades continued to pour in as aespa was crowned as the brand ambassadors for Givenchy on February 10, marking a historic moment as the first K-pop group to represent the renowned French luxury fashion line. The glamour extended to the pages of DAZED Korea's March 2021 issue, showcasing aespa's undeniable presence in the fashion world.

Recognition transcended borders as People Magazine named aespa one of the "Talented Emerging Artists Making Their Mark on the Musical Landscape in 2021" on March 23. The announcement of their first mini-album slated for the summer left fans buzzing with excitement.

May brought fresh blooms of anticipation as SM Entertainment confirmed aespa's comeback. The unveiling of "Next Level" on May 17 captured hearts, promising a thrilling ride with concept photos gracing the days from May 7 to May 15. The press conference revealed plans for their first full-length album, adding an extra layer of excitement to the musical journey.

August ushered in a new era of beauty partnerships, with aespa becoming the face of Clio and later being announced as models for Mediheal and Eider. The world took note of aespa's influence in the realms of fashion and beauty. The brand partnerships continued to flourish, with aespa representing KB Bukopin, Liiv, and Acqua de Parma, solidifying their role as trendsetters in the industry.

September set the stage ablaze with the teaser "Savage." SM Entertainment confirmed the release of aespa's first mini-album, "Savage," on October 5, unleashing a captivating series of concept photos from September 23. November saw aespa grace the Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade stage with a spellbinding performance of "Savage," enchanting audiences worldwide.

Closing the year on a magical note, aespa's participation in the eighth SMTOWN winter album and the release of the heartwarming remake "Dreams Come True" on December 20 added a touch of nostalgia and warmth to the holiday season.


2022: aespa's Sonic Odyssey – From "GOT the beat" to Coachella Debut, "Girls," and Global Triumphs!

On December 27, 2021, the magic began as Karina and Winter were revealed as the dynamic duo of the first unit, "GOT the beat," within the extraordinary project group, Girls On Top. The groundbreaking journey kicked off with the debut single "Step Back" on January 3, 2022.

April 19, 2022, marked a historic moment as SM Entertainment proudly announced aespa's participation in the second week of the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival – a prestigious stage where they became the third K-pop girl group to shine. aespa left an indelible mark with a captivating performance, including the unveiling of the previously unreleased English song, "Life's Too Short."

The accolades poured in, with TIME Magazine's Next Generation Leaders list on May 12 and Forbes' "30 Under 30" list in the Entertainment and Sports category on May 27, solidifying aespa's position as global icons.

The symphony continued on June 1 with the revelation of aespa's second mini-album, "Girls," set to enchant the world on July 8. A major announcement followed as the group signed a monumental contract with the esteemed American record label, Warner Records. In celebration of this partnership, the first English single, "Life's Too Short," was set to captivate hearts on June 24.

aespa expanded their horizons with the announcement of their first Japanese showcase tour, "aespa Japan Premium Showcase 2022 SYNK," scheduled for August 6 and 7 in Yokohama, Japan. Fans in the United States were in for a treat as aespa unveiled plans for their first-ever U.S. showcase, "SYNK in LA," at the YouTube Theater on June 26, with an additional show added due to overwhelming demand.

Amidst promotions for their English single "Life's Too Short," aespa stunned the world with teasers for their highly-anticipated Korean comeback. The culmination of this excitement arrived on July 8, 2022, with the release of the second mini-album "Girls" and its mesmerizing music video. The album soared to unprecedented heights, claiming the top spot in monthly sales with an astonishing 1,645,255 copies sold, according to Circle Chart's monthly update for July.

September 1 brought yet another triumph as Chopard selected aespa as their new global ambassador, a testament to the group's influence in the world of fashion and luxury. November 23 unveiled a teaser poster on the SM C&C Studio Twitter account, heralding the arrival of aespa's first reality show, "Synk Road," set to premiere in December 2022.


2023: aespa's Sonic Odyssey – From "GOT the beat" to Coachella Debut, "Girls," and Global Triumphs!

January 17 marked a historic moment as aespa announced their participation in the Governors Ball music festival, making them the first-ever K-pop group to grace the esteemed event.

January 20 brought a poster of dreams as aespa unveiled their inaugural concert, '2023 aespa 1st Concert 'SYNK : HYPER LINE,'' set to enchant fans on February 25 and 26 at Jamsil Arena. The excitement spread globally as additional Japanese dates were revealed, promising an unforgettable experience.

March 16 ignited anticipation as Star News reported aespa's upcoming album in early May. Within the hour, SM Entertainment confirmed the exciting news, leaving fans on the edge of their seats.

April 14 set hearts racing as Sport TV News reported aespa's imminent comeback on May 8, with the group in the final stages of preparation. The anticipation reached a crescendo on April 17 with the announcement of their third mini album, "MY WORLD," accompanied by a pre-release single, "Welcome To MY World," scheduled for May 2.

July 31 brought another musical delight as aespa revealed "Better Things," set to captivate ears on August 18, adding to the vibrancy of their discography.

October 11 heralded the announcement of aespa's fourth mini album, "Drama," set to unfold its captivating narrative on November 10, promising a theatrical experience for fans worldwide.

Festive cheer graced aespa's journey on November 24 with the release of their third English digital single, "Jingle Bell Rock" – a Christmas remix of the timeless original, spreading joy and warmth to fans around the globe.
